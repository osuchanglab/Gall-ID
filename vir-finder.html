Vir-Search

Search for virulence genes in whole genome sequencing read data.

Agrobacterium and Rhodococcus Virulence Gene Search

Select your organism of interest, whether you have paired- or single-end reads, and drop your sequencing read files onto the target. Your read files will be uploaded and then analyzed using Short Read Sequence Typing (SRST2, ref) to identify known virulence genes.
